About RAMICS

RAMICS is a cutting-edge research project exploring the frontiers of embodied artificial intelligence in multi-agent systems. Our focus is on developing responsible and adaptive robotic systems that can effectively navigate and operate in complex, real-world environments.

Embodied AI

Developing intelligent systems that interact with the physical world through robotic embodiment

Multi-Agent Systems

Creating collaborative networks of intelligent agents that work together to solve complex tasks

Responsible AI

Ensuring ethical considerations and safety in adaptive intelligent systems
